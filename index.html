Anthony Gagnor

Fuse Performance and Rehab

Fuse Performance and Rehab is a concierge, cash-based wellness clinic co-owned by myself (a physical therapist) and my business partner Jordan, a chiropractor who will soon be joining Koda. Our clinic is located in the front offices of Koda CrossFit Iron View.

I provide one-on-one, individualized care focused on injury prevention, rehabilitation, and performance recovery. My treatments and services include dry needling, joint mobilizations and manipulations, soft tissue mobilization, manual cupping, Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation (PNF) techniques and stretching, exercise modification, and tailored exercise programming.

I hold certifications in ICE (Institute of Clinical Excellence) Dry Needling for both the upper and lower body, as well as the cert-CMFA (Clinical Management of the Fitness Athlete), which specializes in treating CrossFit, weightlifting, and powerlifting athletes. Additionally, I am CF-L1 certified and a Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist (CSCS).

Discounted package rates for multiple sessions.
